Hello, my name is Marit and I'm partaking in the course HCI Technologies. This is my second year of Communication and Multi Media Design.

My Expectations

When I was doing a minor in Zoetermeer, I spoke to someone who had already done the HCI weeks. So, I already know we are going to work two straight weeks on a project of our own choice and that we will have an exposition of this product on the last day.

But what I still don't know is what will happen in the weeks before. We were told there will be listening to presentations about different type of technologies, I think we will need to do preparing work with these technologies and that we will be doing that in the workshops.

What I want to learn

Well, ofcourse I want to learn about the different kind of technologies and how to use them. But I also know there are a lot of great projects that came out of the HCI weeks and I would like to take part in such a project and learn how this is done.
