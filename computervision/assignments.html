Computer Vision is working with your computer and creating stuff. I know, I did not know what to expect either. But after the workshop, I found out you really can create a lot of stuff, which is cool. Not only shapes, but you can detect things and shape those.

Assignment 1

We started of with an easy assignment, creating shapes. As you can see, those are very clear to read, ellipses. The shapes are made with the numbers between the brakets.

Assignment 2

We extended from only the use of the ellipse, to all kinds of shapes, rectangles, lines, cirles that are cut open. Which was a bit harder, but we made it.

Assignment 3

Now, things got interactive. We needed to create a shape, that would change with the movement of ymour mouse. My rectangle changed color when the mouse moved. This was very simple, you just needed to use mouseX and mouseY for the colors, as you can see, after fill.

Assignment CV1

We got to the real stuff. Computer Vision. We started of easy, to detect a face and put a green circle around it. In the assignment we got tips, for this one, it was to use CASCADE_FRONTALFACE. When you use that, it will detect the whole face.

Assignment CV2

After we got the hang of it, we needed to make someone unrecognizable. Instead of a black block before your eyes, I used red dots and CASCADE_EYE.

Assignment CV3

Last but not least, something fun. I always liked Pippi Longstocking and this was one of the things that popped in my mind. So, when you step in front of the camera, you turn in the a black and white Pippi Longstocking.
