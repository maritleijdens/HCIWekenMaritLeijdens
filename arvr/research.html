Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality are becoming very popular lately and therefore you can find a lot of examples of this theory. On this page I will show projects that I found very inspiring and will explain the theory shotly.

Street Smarts VR

This form of VR is used as VR training that is used by police departments to help get their officers a better judgement and reduce avoidable police-citizen violence. They use Photorealistic Virtual Reality, so the scenario's are highly realistic and perfect to train in.

Above you can see the solution they had for the three problems. Lack of immersive medium is a problem that is solved by VR Delivery, infrequent training, going to the gunrange once or twice a year, is now solved by headsets that are in the precincts for everyday use. And low probability scenario's, like the taliban walking into Starbucks, will be everyday scenario's. This way, the officers are better prepared in the real world.

They use a lot of "simple" equipment for their VR World. As shown on the picture below you can see they use VR-glasses, earphones and a VR gun. However, we don't know what they use as software.

I found this very interesting, mainly because it is always on the news, about policemen shooting citizens in the US. You hear is a lot these days and I wonder if this will help reducing the numbers of unnecessary gunviolence. So, I guess I'm more interested in the outcome, but I do think this is something that can help with those problems.
