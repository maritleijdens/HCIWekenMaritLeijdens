My expectations

AR & VR has been discussed a lot the past years, not only at school, but also at home. My little brother got VR-glasses for his birthday and played some games on it. I know the AR&VR if it comes with the glasses, but I think in the lecture we will hear about all different kinds of Augmented and Virtual Reality, and not only the one where you have to wear glasses.

My impression

There were a lot of different kinds of versions of augmented and virtual reality, think of in the art museum or the lens. Those are definitely not used with VR-glasses. I found it interesting that there are already lots of different types that are being used. Most of them are used for entertainment, but not the same kind of entertainment. For example, the non-existing lens and the art are both entertainment, but the lens is for using it to play and the art is for looking at it.

Applications & Future

I think the AR&VR will expand to a less entertaining role in the society, it can be used for a lot more, like learning. Kids these days like gaming a lot and with this type of reality it may be more attractive to the children if it comes in a way that makes it look like a game.
