Robotic Fish

In South-Korea they made an artificial creature, a robotic fish. This fish is used to clean the water it swims in, so that people donâ€™t have to take out water first and test it, the fish can determine it by itself and clean it at the same time. It is called the Essex Robotic Fish and was made by Jindong Liu a PhD student. He designed and coded a 3D robotic fish simulator, so he could evaluate the control algorithm of the robotic fish. For the bio-mimetic control, he built a novel kinematical model and implanted that.
